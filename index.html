High-Yield Notes / Study

Click-to-expand study boxes, PDFs, images, notes, or custom mini-apps

💡 Interactive Drilldown is the main type — it shows up on the Study page as a box (e.g. "Reports Interpretator" or "Quick Pharma Revision"). Students click it, it expands right there, they pick a chapter/category, then an item, and the content unfolds below. Ask Claude to build this for you — just describe what you want, and tell Claude to "format it as a QzMED structured study module JSON". Claude will hand you a .json file — upload it here and it's live.

Example prompt to send Claude: "Make me a QzMED structured study module JSON called 'Quick Pharma Revision' with chapters (Antibiotics, Antihypertensives, etc), each containing drugs, each drug with Uses / Dosage / Side Effects sections."

🧩 Custom App is for when you want something more custom-coded than a drilldown (interactive calculators, quizzes-within-quizzes, etc) — Claude builds it as a small self-contained web app and you upload the file it gives you.
